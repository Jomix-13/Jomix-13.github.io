About Me

Hi! My name is John and I'm a full-stack software engineer with a background in accounting and finance. In 2006 I got my bachelor's in accounting and finance.

Since high school I really loved solving problems, I used to do excel programs using functions and built-in tools. I visited four different successful careers in my 15 years of experience, but I always was inspired by my friends who are developers.

Over the last six months, I followed my passion and completed a 1,000+ hour training program at App Academy where I honed my skills in JavaScript, Python, React / Redux, PostgreSQL, and other tools.
